About ICICI Securities Limited

ICICI Securities Limited (I-Sec) is a subsidiary of ICICI Bank Ltd. The company began operations in May 1995 and has continued to grow by expanding its client base and providing different types of services.

I-Sec operates institutional accounts, meeting three major client needs - investment, protection, and IPO applications. I-Sec provides investment services to various types of clients including retail and institutional investors, corporations, high-net-worth individuals, and government institutions through its three main business segments - brokerage, financial product distribution, and wealth management.
